Parameters illustration of Lineage client interface in conf/interface_fishing.au3
file.

1. kFishingWindowLeft - this is left-up point of the "Fishing" window
2. kFishingWindowRight - this is right-bottom point of the "Fishing" window
3. kFishingColor1 - this is color of the random pixel in the "Fishing" window
4. kFishingColor2 - this is color of the random pixel in the "Fishing" window
5. kFishHealthLeft - this is left-up point of the fish's HP bar
6. kFishHealthRight - this is right-bottom point of the fish's HP bar
7. kFishHealthColor - color of the fish's full HP bar (by default blue)
