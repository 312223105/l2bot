Parameters illustration of Lineage 2 client interface in conf/interface_highfive.au3
file. Picture 1.

1. kTargetWindowPos - coordinates of the target state window
2. kTargetWindowColorBrown - first color to detect target window existance
3. kTargetWindowColorGray - second color to detect target window existance
4. kTargetHealthPos - coordinates of the target's health bar
5. kTargetHealthColor - color of the target's full health bar (by default red)
6. kSelfHealthPos - coordinates of the player's health bar
7. kSelfHealthColor - color of the player's full health bar (by default red)
8. kSelfManaPos - coordinates of the player's mana bar
9. kSelfManaColor - color of the player's full mana bar (by default blue)
